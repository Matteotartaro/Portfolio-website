About Me

I'm Matteo Tartaro

UX/UI Designer Junior
Start2Impact

I'm 23, from Italy - a curious mind with a constant need to create.
Before diving into design I worked in restaurants for years — nothing teaches you speed, teamwork, and reading people better than that.
At the same time I've started playing with Photoshop, Cinema 4D, Illustrator and it quickly turned into something bigger than a hobby: I’ve always been drawn to anything that lets me make something from scratch.
What I love about UX/UI design is the mix of thinking and doing. I like taking messy problems, sketching ideas, breaking things apart, rebuilding them, and understanding why something works (or doesn’t).
I'm also the kind of person who has to try new tools, learn new tricks, and explore what I don’t know. I like to think of myself as a sponge — especially around people who know more than me.
